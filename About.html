About the organization

Helping Hands Organization strive to bring unity within the students of RKValley and try to overcome the problems faced. We mainly concentrate with the problems of physically disabled, orphans, aged ones and students in their emergencies. We started this like a stream but we require an external support to make it mighty ocean to help the society.

Our Achievements...

                           

We have helped 200+ students till now. We raised the funds by organizing the different events in our university.Every single rupee is spent on the health of the students.The HELPING HANDS ORGANIZATION is completely in the hands of students with the administration support.
